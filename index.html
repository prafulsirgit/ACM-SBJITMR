ACM - SBJITMR

SB Jain Institute of Technology and Management and Research (SBJITMR) in Nagpur proudly announces the establishment of its ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) student chapter. This significant milestone reflects the institute's commitment to fostering a dynamic learning environment and promoting excellence in the field of computing. The ACM student chapter at SBJITMR provides a platform for students to engage in various activities, including workshops, seminars, and competitions, aimed at enhancing their technical skills and knowledge. Through this chapter, students have the opportunity to connect with professionals, researchers, and fellow enthusiasts in the computing domain, facilitating a rich exchange of ideas and experiences. SBJITMR's ACM student chapter serves as a catalyst for innovation, collaboration, and the overall development of students passionate about the ever-evolving world of technology.
